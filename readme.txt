This is a calculator with a reminder.
You can choose two mode in the application,if you put your phone as vertical,our app
will show you a simple calculator with some normal functions.When you change your phone
to landscape,it will be a scientific calculator with a reminder.
There is still some problems in our version now, we will try to fix them and update it
in the future.
